5 OF MY DREAM VACATION ZONES

1. NEW YORK

Why I would like to visit New York

New York offers iconic landmarks like Times Square, Central Park, and the Statue of Liberty, world-class museums, Broadway shows, diverse cuisine, and vibrant neighborhoods. Its energy, cultural richness, and endless attractions make it a top vacation destination.

5 Fun activities you can do while in New York

  • Visit Central Park
  • See a Broadway Show
  • Tour the Statue of Liberty
  • Explore the Met or MoMA
  • Walk the High Line

2. BARCELONA

Why I would like to visit Barcelona

Barcelona boasts stunning architecture like Gaudí's Sagrada Família, vibrant beaches, lively markets, delicious tapas, and a rich cultural scene. Its mix of history, art, and Mediterranean charm makes it a perfect vacation destination.

5 Fun activities you can do while in Barcelona

  • Catch a football game at Camp Nou
  • Stroll La Rambla
  • Relax at Barceloneta Beach
  • Visit Park Güell
  • Tour the Gothic Quarter

3. ROME

Why I would like to visit Rome

Rome is a treasure trove of ancient history, home to the Colosseum, Roman Forum, and Pantheon. Its vibrant piazzas, delicious cuisine, and iconic landmarks like the Vatican and Trevi Fountain make it a captivating destination for culture, art, and romance.

5 Fun activities you can do while in Rome

  • Visit the Colosseum
  • Toss a Coin in Trevi Fountain
  • Tour the Vatican
  • Wander the Roman Forum
  • Enjoy Italian Gelato

4. PARIS

Why I would like to visit Paris

Paris charms with iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and Notre-Dame. Its romantic ambiance, world-class cuisine, chic boutiques, and artistic heritage make it a timeless destination for culture, history, and unforgettable experiences.

5 Fun activities you can do while in Paris

  • Visit the Eiffel Tower
  • Explore the Louvre
  • Stroll Montmartre
  • Cruise the Seine
  • Indulge in French Pastries

5. THE GREAT WALL OF CHINA

Why I would like to visit The Great Wall of China

The Great Wall of China offers awe-inspiring views, rich history, and incredible hiking opportunities. Stretching across rugged landscapes, it's a UNESCO World Heritage site and one of the world's most iconic landmarks, providing a unique blend of adventure, culture, and breathtaking scenery.

5 Fun activities you can do while at the Great Wall of China

  • Hike Iconic Sections like Mutianyu
  • Take a Cable Car Ride
  • Capture Stunning Photos
  • Visit Watchtowers
  • Toboggan Down
